Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can now register for free! Click Register in the navigation or visit the Login page and click "Register here" to create your account. You'll need to provide your name, email, and choose a username and password.

How do I submit an article?

After registering, you can comment on articles immediately. To submit articles, you need admin approval to become an editor. Contact us to request editor privileges, then you can create articles from your dashboard.

How long does it take for articles to be published?

Articles are typically reviewed and published within 24-48 hours during business days.

Can I edit my published articles?

Yes, you can edit your own articles. Go to your dashboard and click "Edit" next to the article you want to modify.

What's the difference between Reader and Editor accounts?

Reader accounts (free registration) can comment on articles, like articles, and browse all content. Editor accounts (admin approval required) can also create and publish articles.

How do I report inappropriate content?

If you find inappropriate content, please contact us immediately via email or phone with the article details.
